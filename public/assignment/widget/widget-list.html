Tasty is a BuzzFeed division that produces and shares content related to comfort food. Tasty is mostly known for recipe video series posted on its Facebook page. The page is self-described as “Food that'll make you close your eyes, lean back, and whisper "yessss." Snack-sized videos and recipes you'll want to try.” [1] Tasty Facebook page was launched on July 31, 2015, and has over 77 million followers as of December 2016.[1] According to BuzzFeed, every fourth Facebook user watches Tasty recipe approximately once a month. Tasty recipes have received over 1.5 billion views on Facebook in October 2016, making the page 4th most watched Facebook publisher and #1 channel in a culinary genre.[2] The department is responsible for “37 percent of BuzzFeed`s video views”.
